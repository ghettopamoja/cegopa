About C.E.G.O.P.A

The Centre for Governance and Policy in Africa (CEGOPA) aims to contribute to informed public policy for better governance in Africa. We are committed to driving excellence in public policy and governance through provision of the highest standards of research, executive education, customised training and advocacy.

Our mission is to provide innovative solutions to governance challenges...(hover me to continue)

Additional Information

  1. Propose evidence-based solutions to major policy challenges facing the people, businesses and governments in Africa.
  2. Investigate and analyse questions relating to public policy and disseminate research findings to key stakeholders including governments, the media, the private sector, development agencies and the general public with a view to shaping policy outcomes.
  3. Design and implement projects that demonstrate best practices in policy, management and effective governance for the public good.
  4. Conduct trainings for professionals in the public, private and not-for-profit sectors by expanding and deepening their knowledge of public affairs issues, policy design and implementation as well as leadership and governance.

We believe in fostering collaboration and partnerships for sustainable development...(hover me to continue)

Additional Information

Collaboration and partnerships are at the core of our values. By working together with various stakeholders, including governments, businesses, and communities, we aim to achieve sustainable development goals. Our initiatives focus on environmental conservation, governance reforms, and building resilient partnerships to address global challenges.

Feel free to explore our projects and initiatives to learn more about how we're making a positive impact!

Our vision is to be a leading catalyst for positive change in governance across Africa...(hover me to continue)

Additional Information

At CEGOPA, we are committed to driving positive change in governance across Africa. Our vision encompasses promoting transparency, accountability, and inclusivity in governance structures. We work closely with governments, civil society organizations, and international partners to implement effective governance reforms.

Through capacity building, policy advocacy, and innovative solutions, we strive to create a more equitable and democratic society where everyone has a voice in decision-making processes.

We strive to empower individuals and communities through knowledge and advocacy...(hover me to continue)

Additional Information

At CEGOPA, our mission is to empower individuals and communities by providing them with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to drive positive change. Through our advocacy efforts, we aim to amplify the voices of marginalized groups and advocate for policies that promote social justice, equality, and human rights.

Education is at the core of our approach, and we believe that informed citizens are key to building resilient and sustainable societies. By fostering collaboration and partnerships, we work towards creating inclusive environments where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

Additional Information

At CEGOPA, we uphold the principles of integrity, transparency, and accountability in all aspects of our work. We believe that trust is the foundation of effective governance and strive to maintain the highest ethical standards in everything we do.

By promoting transparency, we ensure that our actions are open to scrutiny and accountability, holding ourselves and others to the highest standards of conduct. Our commitment to integrity guides our decision-making processes and strengthens the trust of our stakeholders.

We are dedicated to fostering a culture of excellence and continuous learning...(hover me to continue)

Additional Information

At CEGOPA, we are dedicated to fostering a culture of excellence and continuous learning. We believe that excellence is not a destination but a journey, and we are committed to constantly improving and innovating in all areas of our work.

Through ongoing education and professional development opportunities, we empower our team members to reach their full potential and stay abreast of the latest developments in their fields. By embracing a growth mindset and encouraging feedback, we create an environment where everyone can thrive and contribute to our collective success.
